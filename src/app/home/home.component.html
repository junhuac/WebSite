What Is TIACOIN?

TIACOIN is the future of Insured Cryptocurrency Trading. It is an ecosystem where:
  1. Investors can purchase “Smart Bonds” of a Fund be Insured
  2. Fund Managers can issue “Smart Bonds” and Insure their Bonds
  3. Investors & Fund Managers decide on Trades as a Team
  4. Token Holders get a guaranteed % ROI on all interparty deals
In order to reach our mission we aim to provide an investment platform with all the required tools, agents and infrastructure in order to identify the very best investment opportunities in cryptocurrency markets. We then facilitate arbitrage trading in cryptocurrencies using and maximizing profit taking.

How It Work

Below we shall explain how our system works and how it is different from the current Lending MLM platforms.
In order to better understand our ecosystem, lets first examine how the current Lending MLM platforms operate.
  • 1 Users Invest into the platform by first purchasing the platform’s Tokens in exchange for one of the major cryptocurrencies.
  • 2 The users then invest by lending out their tokens in return for high %ROI.
  • 3 The platform is supposedly able to yield high returns on investment using Trading Bots.
  • 4 The trading bots then supposedly make trades riding on market volatility and yield profits in excess of 5% daily.
  • 5 Out of the +5% capital gained, 1% daily interest is rewarded to Token holders.
In addition to the above mentioned scheme, the Invested Tokens are held as collateral for a period between 3 month up to 1 year.. Finally there is also a Binary MLM used in order recruit and draw in new investors and pumping in new money.
Let’s now demonstrate our solution. To begin with, our system would effectively tackle many of the above mentioned issues and resolve them. Our platform is designed to pay .8% fixed daily interest to its Token Holders. All transactions are done in total transparency and recorded on the blockchain. Here is how our system works:
  • 1 Investors can choose from a variety of Investment Funds. They exchange their Bitcoin for “Smart Bonds”. “Smart Bonds” are “Smart Contracts” issued by Fund Managers.
  • 2 Managers can choose to insure their Funds by purchasing TIA Coins which will be held in escrow as collateral by the Oracle.
  • 3 The Fund managers are provided with Trading Bots running a variety of Algorithms. Different Bots can be selected, and optimized based upon the marketing strategies of the Fund Manager.
  • 4 The Bots make “Instant Investment Alerts” regarding market conditions and inform investors through our “Mobile Application”.
  • 5 Once Crowd Consensus is reached the Trade Oracle executes the trade. All executed Trades are recorded on the blockchain. 2.4% Transaction fee deducted daily.

TIA Platform Total Profit Distribution

The platform distributes the 2.4% daily profits as follows:
  • 0.8% Guaranteed daily ROI distributed to all Token Holders on daily basis including Weekends & Holidays
  • 0.8% is invested on Silver Holdings to Back the Tokens By Physical Silver deliverable upon request of Token Holders
  • 0.4% Invested in Promising ICO's Picked through Crowd Consensus, Crowd Investing, and receiving Volume Discounts
  • 0.4% for R D, management and operation costs.
